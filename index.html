Courses we offer

Below are courses we offer:

Higher National Diploma (HND)

HNDs are vocational, work-related higher education qualifications. They are equivalent to the first two years of a university degree and are often pursued by students who want to gain practical skills and knowledge for a specific industry. HNDs are usually offered in areas such as business, engineering, computing, and health and social care.

Degree Courses

Degree courses, also known as undergraduate courses, lead to a Bachelor's degree. They typically last three to four years (full-time) and provide a comprehensive understanding of a subject area. There are different types of degrees, including Bachelor of Arts (BA), Bachelor of Science (BSc), Bachelor of Engineering (BEng), and Bachelor of Education (BEd), among others.

Postgraduate Courses

Postgraduate courses are for students who have already completed a Bachelor's degree and wish to further their studies in a specific field. Common postgraduate qualifications include Master's degrees (e.g., MA, MSc, MBA) and Doctoral degrees (PhD). These courses offer more specialized knowledge and often involve research or practical project.

Our global campus

Kumasi Campus: Our Kumasi campus is located in the heart of the Ashanti Region, known for its rich cultural heritage and vibrant atmosphere.The campus offers a range of modern facilities, including state-of-the-art lecture halls, libraries, and recreational areas. Students at the Kumasi campus benefit from a supportive academic environment and access to industry partnerships for practical learning opportunities.



Accra Campus: Situated in Ghana's bustling capital city, our Accra campus provides students with a dynamic learning experience in a cosmopolitan setting. The campus features cutting-edge facilities and resources to support academic excellence and personal development. Students in Accra have the advantage of networking with professionals in various industries, enhancing their career prospects.



Obuasi Campus: Our Obuasi campus is located in a vibrant mining town, offering a unique academic environment focused on industry-relevant education. The campus boasts modern facilities and a conducive learning atmosphere, ideal for students pursuing careers in mining, engineering, and related fields. Students at the Obuasi campus benefit from close ties with local industries, providing valuable practical experience and networking opportunities.
