Applied science and technology

The Faculty of Applied Science and Technology offers a range of innovative programs designed to equip students with the practical skills and theoretical knowledge needed to excel in today's rapidly evolving industries. Our faculty is comprised of experienced professionals and researchers who are dedicated to providing a dynamic learning environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration.

Our programs cover a wide range of disciplines, including engineering, computing, and technology, with a focus on hands-on learning and real-world applications. Students have access to state-of-the-art facilities and laboratories, where they can apply their knowledge in a practical setting.
